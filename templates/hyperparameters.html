Hyperparameter Guide

Architecture

Hidden Layers: More layers = higher capacity, but risk of overfitting

Neurons: More neurons = better learning, but slower training

Dropout: Prevents overfitting by randomly setting neurons to 0

Training

Learning Rate: Higher = faster learning, but may overshoot optimal

Batch Size: Larger = stable gradients, but more memory

Epochs: More = better convergence, but risk of overfitting

Advanced

Activation: GELU often performs better than ReLU

Optimizer: AdamW often outperforms Adam with weight decay

Experiment: Try different combinations to find optimal settings
